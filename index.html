Forrest Gump

Starring Tom Hanks

Date: 1994

"Forrest Gump" is a 1994 American drama film directed by Robert Zemeckis, based on the 1986 novel by Winston Groom. The story follows the life of Forrest Gump, a kind-hearted man with a low IQ, played by Tom Hanks. Despite his cognitive limitations, Forrest witnesses and unwittingly influences several defining historical events in the 20th century United States, all while pursuing his deep love for his childhood friend, Jenny. The film explores themes of destiny, love, and the impact of historical moments on individual lives, all underscored by Forrest's unwavering optimism and integrity.
